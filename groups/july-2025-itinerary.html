Tentative Daily Itinerary

12 Days, 10 Nights (1446-1447H)

*This is a tentative schedule and is subject to change.

Day 1 - Friday, 25 July

Arrival in Jeddah & Transfer to Madinah

Arrive at King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED). After immigration, travel by bus to Madinah. Upon arrival, check into your hotel. The rest of the day is for rest.

Day 2 - Saturday, 26 July

Madinah - Inner Tour (Raudhah)

After breakfast, you will visit the interior of Masjid Nabawi, including the Tomb of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), the graves of his companions Abu Bakar and Umar, and the blessed garden, Raudhah.

Day 3 - Sunday, 27 July

Worship in Madinah

Free time for you to perform your own worship at Masjid Nabawi.

Day 4 - Monday, 28 July

Madinah - Outer Historical Tour (Ziarah)

Explore the historical landmarks of Madinah, including Masjid Quba, a date palm grove, Uhud Mountain, Khandaq Mosque, and Masjid Qiblatain.

Day 5 - Tuesday, 29 July

Madinah to Mecca

Free time for worship at Masjid Nabawi. After dinner, the group will begin the journey to Mecca by bus.

Day 6 - Wednesday, 30 July

Arrival in Mecca & Perform Umrah

On the way to Mecca, the group will stop at the Bir Ali miqat point to enter the state of Ihram. Upon arrival in Mecca, check into the hotel, then proceed to perform the rites of Umrah (Tawaf, Sa'i, Tahallul) with the guidance of the Mutawif.

Day 7 - Thursday, 31 July

Worship in Mecca

A full day for you to perform your own worship at Masjidil Haram.

Day 8 - Friday, 01 August

Mecca - Outer Tour (Ja'ranah)

Tour of significant sites around Mecca: Jabal Thur, Jabal Rahmah, Arafah, Muzdalifah & Mina. A stop will be made at Ja'ranah for those who wish to perform a second Umrah.

Day 9 - Saturday, 02 August

Mecca - Hudaibiyah Tour

A tour to Hudaibiyah and Jabal Nur (Cave of Hira'). Opportunity to enter Ihram at Hudaibiyah for another Umrah.

Day 10 - Sunday, 03 August

Farewell Tawaf & Departure Prep

Free time for personal worship and to perform the Farewell Tawaf (Tawaf Wada'). After dinner, prepare your luggage for departure.

Day 11 - Sunday, 03 August

Travel to Jeddah & Depart

Travel to King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED) for your flight home. Depart from Jeddah at 19:25 on Oman Air flight WY 676.

Day 12 - Monday, 04 August

Arrival in Phnom Penh

After transiting through Muscat and Bangkok, you are scheduled to arrive at Phnom Penh International Airport (PNH) at 19:55. Welcome home!

May your Umrah be accepted. We wish you a safe and blessed journey.
