Tentative Daily Itinerary

12 Days, 10 Nights (1446-1447H)

*This is a tentative schedule and is subject to change.

01

24 Jul

Phnom Penh to Madinah

Gather at the airport 5 hours before departure. Travel to Jeddah and transfer by bus to Madinah for check-in and rest.

02

25 Jul

Madinah Outer Ziarah

Visit historical sites: Masjid Quba, Date Farm, Uhud Mountain, Masjid Qiblatain, and Khandak.

03

26 Jul

Madinah Inner Ziarah

Visit Masjid Ghamamah, Masjid Saidina Ali & Abu Bakar, Saqifah Bani Saadah, Baqi' Cemetery, and pray in the Raudhah.

04

27 Jul

Madinah to Makkah

Perform Ziarah Wada'. After Zuhur, travel to Makkah, stopping at Bir Ali for Miqat. Check-in and perform your first Umrah.

05

28 Jul

Worship in Makkah

Free day for personal worship and activities at Masjid al-Haram.

06

29 Jul

Makkah Outer Ziarah

Visit Jabal Tsur, Arafah, Jabal Rahmah, and Miqat at Masjid Jarannah for a second Umrah.

07

30 Jul

Worship in Makkah

Free day for personal worship and activities at Masjid al-Haram.

08

31 Jul

Additional Ziarah

Visit the area of Jabal Nour and perform Miqat for another Umrah at Hudaibiyah.

09

01 Aug

Worship in Makkah

Free day for personal worship and activities at Masjid al-Haram.

10

02 Aug

Preparation for Departure

Free day for worship. A briefing for the journey home will be held after dinner.

11

03 Aug

Makkah to Jeddah Airport

Travel by bus to King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED) for your flight home.

12

04 Aug

Arrival in Phnom Penh

After transiting, you are scheduled to arrive home. In sya Allah.

May your Umrah be accepted. We wish you a safe and blessed journey.
